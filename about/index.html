About

We are Drum Machine Funk, a creative digital workshop and record label based in Budapest, Hungary.

We love to design and implement creative, unconventional digital solutions informed by our love for creative arts, music, playfulness and inclusivity. We love music and everything that is connected to it: vinyl records, electronic and acoustic instruments, drum machines and synthesizers.

If you have a project in mind that you would like to talk to us about feel free to get in touch by contacting us at hello@drummachinefunk.com.
