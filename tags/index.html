SPL-12

The SPL-12 Sampling Percussion Librarian is a companion app for the E-mu SP-12 Sampling Percussion drum machine.The application makes no sound on it’s own. It’s purpuse is to provide a convenient way to save and load sequencer and sound memory data via a MIDI connection.We hope enthusiasts will find this utility useful and keep the spirit of 12-bit sampling alive for many years to come. Enjoy!

Lahmacun Radio

Lahmacun Radio is a Budapest-based online community radio station founded in 2018, showcasing the city’s various subcultures and musical scenes. The station’s studio is located in Kazán Community House, situated in District VIII, on the grounds of a former factory that now hosts numerous cultural and social projects.

Lahmacun Radio works not only with local but also international hosts, including contributors from Brussels, Edinburgh, Berlin, San Francisco, and Belgrade. Additionally, it collaborates with other online radio stations such as Radio Kapital from Poland, Cashmere Radio from Germany, and IDA Radio from Estonia.

Tilos Radio

Tilos Radio is a unique and independent radio station based in Budapest, Hungary, known for its progressive voice and community-oriented approach. Since its inception in the early 1990s, Tilos has championed freedom of speech, diversity of opinion, and artistic experimentation, making it a prominent alternative to mainstream media in the region. Originally broadcasting illegally as a pirate station, Tilos—meaning “forbidden” in Hungarian—has since evolved into a legal and widely respected platform for underground music, social commentary, and grassroots activism.

Label

Dil Maddy - The Archives

Hungary’s renown beatmaker, Dil Maddy presents “The Archives” - a collection of carefully crafted beats from the heart of Budapest. It’s the first time these songs are presented in their full instrumental glory venturing into heavy hitting boom bap bangers, melancholic breakbeat soundscapes and soulful sample puzzles. The tracks include beats from previous collaborations, remixes and one-offs spanning almost a decade in the making.

All tracks have been produced & mixed by Dil Maddy on the MPC2000XL, MPC60, S1000 and S3000 using vinyl samples between 2010 and 2017 in Budapest, Hungary.
