SL950

Control your AKAI S950 from your iPad!

SL950 is an iPad and iPhone application that connects to your AKAI S950 sampler over a MIDI connection and let’s you edit all parameters of the programs and samples. Speed up your workflow by creating and generating keygroups and chopping up samples!

Features

  • Edit program and sample parameters
  • Chop breaks and upload samples
