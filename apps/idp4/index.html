Control your Ensoniq DP/4 from your iPad

iDP/4 is an iPad application that connects to your Ensoniq DP/4 over a MIDI connection and let’s you edit all parameters of the 4 effect blocks as well as the routing between them.

Features

  • Edit all effect block parameters
  • Control multiple parameters from the touchpad surface
