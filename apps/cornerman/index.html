Backup your KO II to your mobile device

Cornerman is a companion app for the Teenage Engineering KO II sampler composer.
The app uses a USB-C connection to save and restore backups from the KO II.

Features

  • Backup and restore all samples and projects
  • Share your backups
  • Browse the guide to learn all the shortcuts
  • Import and trim samples
